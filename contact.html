Got A Good Story?

Flute Watch primarily publishes featured articles of the leading, aspiring, and amateur flutists of our generation.

We are constantly on the lookout for fresh content of any accomplishment, notable event, and interesting story that involves at least one classical flutist. We love to hear about the wisdom from top professionals, entreprenurial ventures that push the boandaries of classical flute, and stories from student flutists that inspire our readers. If you have a story lead or an article that meets one of these criteria that you would like to contribute, you can contact us here and our publishers will reach out within 24 hours.

Please do note that at this time, we do not provide any compensation for leads or articles. In the future when we look to scale, your content may then be eligible for ad-based revenue. Until then, we provide a chance to feature your social media handles or links.

About Flute Watch

Flute Watch is an online blog and magazine dedicated to covering professional, up-and-coming, and student flutists in the classical music world. We emphasize publishing featured articles that interview the flutists of our generation who are making history.
